❄ Snow & Ice Removal — Quick Reference ❄

{{ business_name }}
⚠ When to Act
  • Snow accumulation reaches 1 inch on walkways
  • Any ice formation on walking surfaces
  • Temperature drops below 32°F after rain or melt
  • Before opening if overnight snow/ice occurred
☑ Removal Checklist
  1. Check conditions at start of shift and every 2 hours
  2. Clear main entrance and exits FIRST
  3. Shovel all walkways and customer paths
  4. Apply de-icer to cleared surfaces
  5. Check for ice under snow — scrape if needed
  6. Place wet floor/caution signs at entrances
  7. Re-check after 2 hours during active weather
⚠ Always Remember
  • LOG every action on the Activity Log
  • Take photos of cleared areas when possible
  • Report any falls or injuries to management IMMEDIATELY
  • Never ignore ice — apply sand/grit if de-icer is unavailable
